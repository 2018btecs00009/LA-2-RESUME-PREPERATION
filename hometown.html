Manerajuri is a Village in Tasgaon Taluka in Sangli District of Maharashtra State, India. It belongs to Desh or Paschim Maharashtra region . It belongs to Pune Division . It is located 26 KM towards North from District head quarters Sangli. 9 KM from Tasgaon. 345 KM from State capital Mumbai Manerajuri Pin code is 416408 and postal head office is Manerajuri .

Manerajuri Local Language is Marathi. Manerajuri Village Total population is 14204 and number of houses are 2869. Female Population is 48.9%. Village literacy rate is 74.4% and the Female Literacy rate is 34.1% .
